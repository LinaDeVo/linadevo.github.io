Survey responses about cultural narratives surrounding disability

Posted by: Survey Participant
If you could reshape one cultural narrative about disability, what would it be?
"I would reshape notions of what disabled people 'can' do. Most disabled people can do most things if made accessible, but sometimes there is no option. Regardless, we should appreciate individuals as people regardless of their ability to do or not do any given thing."
Posted by: Survey Participant
If you could reshape one cultural narrative about disability, what would it be?
"That disability is not something to be frowned upon. It is a struggle, but also something of power."
Posted by: Survey Participant
