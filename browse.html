Capstone Project

Senior Writing Studies Capstone

This project examines how writing functions as a site of identity formation, access, and resistance. Drawing from feminist and disability studies, the essay explores the intersections of personal narrative, theory, and institutional language.

Through archival analysis and reflective practice, the project asks how writing can be both a tool of exclusion and a means of survival.
